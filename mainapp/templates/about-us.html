GUITAR WORLD

Hello, I am Antonio, the founder of this project called "Guitar world". One day I had the intention of looking for many models of guitars only with the need to know more and also to see videos of what it sounded like. I realized that I had to go to multiple web applications already that all of them had information but there was not a single page with all that information gathered, many were paid and I did not feel that they gave me quality information about the instrument, so this need to collect quality information with really demonstrative and honest videos of how they sound in actually the instruments that we musicians like so much.

After coming from a country where it is world-renowned for making guitars by hand, I realized that there is not a website where I collect information not only about guitars from my country but from around the world, through this the need to collect at least one representative model of each country in the world so that more people know not only the commercial models but also the handmade guitars.
